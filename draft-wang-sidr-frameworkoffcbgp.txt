Abstract

   This document defines a standard profile for the framework of
   Forwarding Commitment BGP (FC-BGP).  A FC is a digitally signed
   object that provides a means of verifying that an IP address prefix
   is announced from AS a to AS b.  When validated, a FC's eContent can
   be used for detection and mitigation of route hijacking and provide
   protection for the AS_PATH attribute in BGP-UPDATE.
